Our Services

This app is a digital assistant for bartenders, streamlining cocktail management, ingredient tracking, and customer recommendations.

What the App Offers

  • 🔐 Authentication with GDPR consent and favorite cocktail management
  • 👤 Personal Area: user info and privacy consents
  • 🏠 Home Page showing favorite cocktails or random selection
  • 🔎 Cocktail Search by type, ingredients, or name
  • 🍸 Cocktail Details and ability to save favorites
  • 🛠️ Create and manage custom cocktails
  • 🌐 Universal access to saved recipes from any device
  • ✏️ Edit, update, and delete your custom recipes
  • 🖼️ Upload an image for your custom cocktails
  • 🔤 Multilingual Web App: Italian, English, and French
  • 🧩 User profiling with GDPR consent and adaptive interfaces
  • 📅 "Selection of the Day": curated drink menu updated by admins
  • 📋 Dedicated page and reduced version on home with CTA link
  • 🛡️ Admin Role with access to advanced features
  • 🧃 Create and manage drink groups for the Selection of the Day
  • 👥 Admin-user interaction through active drink selections
